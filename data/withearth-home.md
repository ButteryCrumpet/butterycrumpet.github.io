# Withearth Home Profile

## Company Overview

**Name:** ウィザースホーム (Withearth Home)
**Parent Company:** Shinshowa Group (新昭和グループ)
**Founded:** 1970 (parent company), 2001 (Withearth Home brand), 2021 (standalone company)
**Type:** Mid-tier house maker (中堅ハウスメーカー)
**Specialty:** 2x4 and 2x6 construction method (ツーバイフォー・ツーバイシックス工法)
**Philosophy:** "Creating people, creating homes, and creating a sustainable Earth's future"
**Service Areas:** Kanto region - Chiba, Tokyo, Kanagawa, Saitama, Ibaraki, Yamanashi prefectures

## Company History

### Parent Company Background

**Shinshowa Corporation** was established in April 1970 in Kimitsu City, Chiba Prefecture as Shinshowa Housing Co., Ltd. The company merged several subsidiaries in 1992 and became a comprehensive housing and construction enterprise.

### Withearth Home Brand Evolution

- **2001:** Withearth Home brand launched as Shinshowa's 2x4/2x6 construction housing brand
- **2021:** Shinshowa Withers East Kanto and Shinshowa Withers Kanagawa merged to form standalone Withearth Home Corporation (headquarters: Chiba City)
- **2021:** Celebrated 20th anniversary of the Withearth Home brand

### Market Position

As a **2x4 construction pioneer** through the Shinshowa Group, Withearth Home positions itself as a mid-tier house maker offering:
- Comparable quality to major house makers
- More affordable pricing than premium builders
- Stronger brand recognition than local builders (工務店)

## Core Construction Technology

### 2x4 and 2x6 Construction Method

**Structural System:**

- **2x4 (Two-By-Four):** Standard structural lumber method using 2-inch x 4-inch studs
- **2x6 (Two-By-Six):** Enhanced method using 2-inch x 6-inch studs for superior insulation
- **Box structure:** Six-sided box (floor, walls, ceiling) provides earthquake resistance
- **Panel construction:** Factory-produced components ensure quality control

**Advantages:**

- Superior earthquake resistance through box structure
- Excellent insulation capabilities (especially 2x6)
- Faster construction timeline with factory components
- Quality consistency through standardized manufacturing

## Conclusion

Withearth Home represents a **pragmatic mid-tier choice** offering strong energy efficiency, environmental commitment, and proven construction quality at reasonable pricing. The company's 50+ year parent company history, 2x4/2x6 construction expertise, and comprehensive support systems provide solid fundamentals.

However, for your specific priorities emphasizing **natural light and garden space integration**, Withearth Home lacks the specialized design philosophy and demonstrated expertise that competitors like Sumitomo Forestry or custom architects offer. The company's strength lies in systematic quality and energy performance rather than natural design integration.

**Recommendation:** Consider Withearth Home as a **backup option** or for comparison against specialized builders, particularly valuable if energy efficiency becomes equal priority to natural design, or if budget constraints require mid-tier pricing. Schedule a showroom visit to assess their custom design capabilities, but maintain focus on builders with proven natural light and garden integration expertise as primary candidates.
